    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage: 21st May - 13th June, 2022
      • 8 teams, Single Round Robin
      • All Matches are Bo3
      • 1st & 2nd placed team advances to Upper Bracket Round 2
      • 3rd to 6th placed team advances to Upper Bracket Round 1
    • Playoffs: 18th - 26th June, 2022
      • 6 teams, Double-Elimination bracket
        • All matches (excl. Lower Bracket Final and Grand Final) are Bo3
        • Lower Final and Grand Final are Bo5
      • Winner qualifies for Stage 2 Masters
