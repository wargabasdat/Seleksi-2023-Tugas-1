    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage
      • Round Robin
      • Each group has six teams
      • All matches are Bo3
        • Top 4 teams advance to Playoffs
        • Bottom 2 teams are eliminated
    • Playoffs
      • Double Elimination
      • All matches (excl. Losers' Final and Grand Final) are Bo3
      • Losers' Final and Grand Final are Bo5
      • Finalists advance to Stage 1 Masters
