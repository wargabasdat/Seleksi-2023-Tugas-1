    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage
      • Single Round Robin
      • Each group has five teams
      • All teams play each other once
      • All matches are Bo3
        • Top 3 teams advance to Playoffs
        • Top 1 teams are seeded into Semifinals.
        • Bottom 2 teams advance to Relegation
    • Playoffs
      • Double Elimination
      • All matches (excl. Winners' Finals, Losers' Finals and Grand Final) are Bo3
      • The Winners' Finals, Losers' Finals and Grand Final are Bo5
      • Winner qualifies for Stage 1 Masters
