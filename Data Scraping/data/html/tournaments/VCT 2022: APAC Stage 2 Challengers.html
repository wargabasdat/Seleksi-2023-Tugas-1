    Format[edit]

    • Play-In: June 9th - 12th, 2022
      • Two double-elimination format (GSL) groups
        • 8 Low Seed teams
        • Each group has 4 teams
        • All matches are Bo3
      • Top two teams from each group advance to the Group Stage
    • Group Stage: June 15th - 20th, 2022
      • Four double-elimination format (GSL) groups
        • 12 High Seed teams, 4 Play-In teams
        • Each group has 4 teams
        • All matches are Bo3
      • Top two teams from each group advance to the Knockouts
    • Knockouts: June 23rd - 26th, 2022
      • Double-Elimination bracket
      • 1st place vs 2nd place from each group. Randomized seeding.
        • Teams from the same group could not be drawn into the same side of the bracket
        • All matches are (excl. Grand Final) are Bo3
        • Grand Final is Bo5
      • Top two teams will qualify for Stage 2 Masters
