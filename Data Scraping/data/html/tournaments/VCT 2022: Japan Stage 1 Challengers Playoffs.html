    Format[edit]

    • Double Elimination bracket.
      • All matches (excl. Grand Final) are Bo3
      • Grand Final is Bo5
      • Click here for a detailed explanation of the Playoffs Seeding
    Pool Teams
    Pool 1 REJECT ZETA DIVISION
    Pool 2 IGZIST Crazy Raccoon Northeption Crest Gaming
    Pool 3 FAV gaming Reignite
    • Teams from the Pool 1 & 3 will be seeded in UB R1 Match 1 and Match 3
    • Teams from the Pool 2 will be seeded in UB R1 Match 2 and Match 4
    • Winner qualifies for Stage 1 Masters
