    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage:
      • Round Robin
      • Each group has six teams
      • All matches are Bo3
        • Top 4 teams advance to Playoffs
        • Bottom 2 teams are eliminated
    • Playoffs: 8 teams in a Double-Elimination bracket
      • All matches (excl. Upper , Losers' Final and Grand Final) are Bo3
      • Upper , Losers' Final and Grand Final are Bo5
      • Finalists will advance to Stage 2 Masters
