    Format[edit]

    • Play-In: VRL teams in 2 groups of four teams in a GSL format.
      • All matches are Bo3
    • Promotion Stage: 6 teams in a double-elimination bracket
      • The two VCT teams receive a bye in the first round
      • All matches (excl. Lower Bracket final) are Bo3
      • Lower Bracket final is Bo5
      • Grand Final will not be played
