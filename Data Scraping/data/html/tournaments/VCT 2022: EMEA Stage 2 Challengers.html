    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage: 2 groups of six teams in a Round Robin format
      • Top 3 teams advance to Playoffs
      • Top team has a one round bye into the Playoffs.
      • All matches are Bo3
    • Playoffs: 6 teams in a Double-Elimination bracket
      • All matches (excl. Lower Bracket Final and Grand Final) are Bo3
      • Lower Bracket Final and Grand Final are Bo5
      • Top 3 teams qualify for Stage 2 Masters
