    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage: February 10th - 20th, 2022
      • Four round-robin format groups
      • All matches are Bo3
      • Top 2 teams from each group advances to Playoffs
      • Click here for tiebreaker rules
    1. Map win / lost
    2. Round difference
    3. Tiebreaker match
    • Playoffs: February 24th - 27th, 2022
      • Single-Elimination bracket
      • All matches (excl. Grand Final and 3rd Place Match) are Bo3
      • Grand Final and 3rd Place Match are Bo5
