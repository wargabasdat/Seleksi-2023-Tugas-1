    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage:
      • Four double-elimination format (GSL) groups
        • Each group has 4 teams
        • All matches are Bo3
      • Top 2 teams from each group advances to Playoffs
    • Playoffs:
      • Double-Elimination bracket
      • All matches (excl. LB Round 1, LB Round 2 and Grand Final) are Bo3
      • LB Round 1 & LB Round 2 are Bo1
      • Grand Final is Bo5
