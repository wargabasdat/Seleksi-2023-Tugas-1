    Broadcast Talent[edit]

    • Commentators:
      •  Kant (Kao Wei-teng)
      •  AsSen (Huang Mao-sen)
      •  Nia (Fu Kuan-chun)

      •  Rex (Lin Jinan)
      •  Kmomo (Hsu Mao-chun)
      •  Krapy (Liao Hsien-ting)

    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage:
      • 8 teams, Single Round Robin format
      • All matches are Bo3
      • Top 6 teams advances to Playoffs
    • Playoffs:
      • Double-elimination bracket
      • All matches (excl. Grandfinal) are Bo3
      • Grand Final is Bo5
