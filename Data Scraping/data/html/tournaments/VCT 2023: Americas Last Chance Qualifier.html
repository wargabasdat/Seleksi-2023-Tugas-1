    Format[edit]

    • Playoffs: July 15th - 23th, 2023
      • 7 teams Hybrid elimination format
        • 6th and 7th seeded teams compete in Round 1 (Single-Elimination)
        • 3rd - 5th seeded teams have a bye into the Quarterfinals (Single-Elimination)
        • 1st and 2nd seeded teams have a bye into the Upper Bracket Semifinals
        • Upper Bracket Semifinals and onwards is Double-Elimination
      • All matches (excl. Lower Final and Grand Final) are Bo3
      • Lower Final and Grand Final are Bo5
      • Deadlock is not available in this event.
