    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage: 2 groups of four teams in a Double-Elimination format
      • Top 2 teams advance to Playoffs
      • First winner matches are Bo1
      • All others matches are Bo3
    • Finals: 2 matches played for seeding
      • Winner of each groups face in a Bo3
      • Runner-up of each groups face in a Bo1
