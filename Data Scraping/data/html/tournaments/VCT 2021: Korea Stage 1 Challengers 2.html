    Broadcast Talent[edit]

    • Korean Casters:
      •  최광원 (Choi Kwang-won)
      •  채민준 (Chae Min-jun)
      •  김수현 (Kim Su-hyeon)
    • Korean Commentators:
      •  정인호 (Jung In-ho)
      •  BINBON (Kim Jin-young)
      •  Dingception (Jang Kwang-myun)
    • English Commentators:
      •  Achilios (Seth King)
      •  Paperthin (Clinton Bader)
      •  Tasteless (Nicolas Plott)
      •  Artosis (Dan Stemkoski)

    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage: 2 groups of 4 teams in a Round Robin format
      • Top 2 teams of each group advance to Masters
      • Bottom 2 teams of each group advance to Challengers 3
      • All matches are Bo2
