    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage: 2 groups of four teams in a GSL format
      • Top 2 teams advance to Playoffs
      • All matches are Bo3
    • Playoffs: 4 team Single-Elimination bracket
      • Semifinals are Bo3
      • Grand Final is Bo5
