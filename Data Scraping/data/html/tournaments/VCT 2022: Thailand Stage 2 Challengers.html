    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage:
      • Single Round Robin
        • 8 teams (4 invited teams and 4 closed qualified teams)
        • All matches are Bo3
      • Top 6 teams advances to Playoffs
      • Click here for tiebreaker rules
    1. Map difference
    2. Round difference
    • Playoffs:
      • 6 teams, Double-elimination bracket
      • All matches (excl. Grand Final) are Bo3
      • Grand Final is Bo5
